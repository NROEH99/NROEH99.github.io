Movies

Howls Moving Castle:
Howl's Moving Castle will always have a special place in my heart as my dad showed it to me when I was a kid and
we have continued to bond over movies alot throughout my life. It was the first Studio Ghibli film that I watched
and it will always be my favorite. The worlds that Hayao Miyazaki creates are so intricate and beautiful they will
draw you in and never let you go.

Spirited Away:
Yet another of Hayao Miyazaki's masterpieces, Spirited Away is another one of my all time favorites. Maybe an even
more beautiful world than the world of Howl's, Spirited Away is truly one of a kind. From the creepy corners to the
majestic landscapes this film never ceases to amaze me no matter how many times I go back for more.

Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back:
Now on to Star Wars. There is something about a world that seems never ending in its lore that entices me so. From
an animated universe to a galaxy far far away it is these vast always growing worlds that I eat right up. Yes the
Star Wars fandom can be extremely toxic (and I mean all the time!) but we can all unite under our common love for
the lands and inhabitants of the Star Wars universe. There is just so much to be a fan of in Star Wars, from the vehicles,
to the classic lightsabers, to the different characters and force abilities.
