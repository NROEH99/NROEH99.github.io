Tv Shows

One Piece:
I started watching One Piece earlier this year and it has become my favorite show already. Almost 400 episodes in
and I still want more (thankfully for me there is over 600 episodes and the entire manga to go). Never did I think
that I would be capable of watching a show that is over 1000 episodes long in it's entirety. Yet here I am. Of all
the anime that I have watched this one is definitely the most fun. There are so many characters to love and so many
ups and downs along the way, which will play with your emotions and have you feeling as if you were apart of the crew.
What I enjoy the most though, is seeing how Luffy and his crew will overcome each new obstacle and each new ever
increaisngly stronger enemy that stands in their path towards greatness. I always find myself yearning for the next
adventure and the next big fight.

It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia:
A welcome break from all the Star Wars content and anime that I consume, Sunny never fails to make me laugh. Every
character has the own comedic style and timing which makes for a great time. Whether it's Dennis being a psychopath
or Frank being Frank I always know I can watch this show to get a laugh.

Andor:
Out of all the live action Star Wars shows, Andor comes out on top. Even though there are no figurehead characters
involved it keeps you engaged and craving more. I have watched all of the Mandolorian, Boba Fett, Obi-Wan, and Ahsoka.
However, Andor had me on the edge of my seat and fully engaged till the bitter end!
